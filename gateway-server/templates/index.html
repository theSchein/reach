What do we do?

Reach facilitates a connection between those with substance abuse issues and Certified Recovery Specialists. Reach can facilitate the warm handoff, but more importantly, it can facilitate what people in recovery need, not a warm handoff, but a recovery coach.

How does it work?

Individual consents to connecting with a Certified Recovery Specialist through Reach.

Reach connects the CRS directly to the individual through SMS.

The CRS coordinates meeting with the patient as early as possible. The Individual and CRS develop a plan and create some goals.

The CRS follows up on the individual to help them achieve their goals.
